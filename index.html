Technical Round
1. What are the key responsibilities of a Program Manager?
The key responsibilities include planning, executing, and monitoring programs, ensuring alignment with business goals, and managing cross-functional teams.
2. How do you prioritize tasks in a program?
Prioritization is based on factors such as business impact, urgency, resource availability, and alignment with strategic goals.

Technical Round
1. What is the role of a Scrum Master in Agile?
A Scrum Master facilitates the Scrum process, removes impediments, and ensures the team adheres to Agile principles.
2. How do you ensure team collaboration?
By promoting open communication, conducting regular stand-ups, and fostering a culture of trust and transparency.
